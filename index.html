Research Summary

Computer Vision

My key research areas are medical image analysis, satellite image analysis (remote sensing) and video analytics

Machine Learning

Machine learning methods are a core part of all my research project. In terms of ML research I'm interested in few-shot learning, continual learning, explainable AI and AI safety

Data Science

There are several data science projects (on going and finished) ranging from medical applications to preventive diagnostics and business intelligence applications I have mainly worked with undergraduate and postgraduate students

Robotics and IoT

From my time as cofounder of Enway GmbH, Berlin, Germany, I have been working on several robotics and IoT related projects ranging from real-time object detection on embedded hardware to smart agriculture and manufacturing applications
